Thousands of customers trust Tulipink to deliver flowers with care, and to keep their personal information private and secure. We take that trust seriously.

Data Security

All customer information and transaction data on Tulipink are securely stored using trusted cloud infrastructure hosted by Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the United States. We use AWS firewalls and monitoring tools to safeguard against unauthorized access and system vulnerabilities. Sensitive data, such as order details and payment information, are encrypted at rest and protected using industry-standard security practices. For more about AWS security, visit AWS Security Center.

Tulipink never shares your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent. While we manage the platform and its databases, you retain full ownership of your data.

Data Reliability

To ensure your data is always safe, all Tulipink customer data is regularly backed up and stored securely on AWS servers. These backups allow for quick restoration in case of unexpected issues. You may also request a backup of your order history by contacting our support team.

Breach Notification

If there is ever a data breach that could impact your privacy or data security, Tulipink will notify affected users within 72 hours through official emails or notifications. We are committed to transparency and proactive communication in such rare events.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Tulipink is transparent about the data we collect and how we use it. To learn more about our data collection practices and your rights, please review our full Privacy Policy.
