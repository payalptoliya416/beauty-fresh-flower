Bringing Joy to Doorsteps, One Flower at a Time

At Tulipink, we believe every flower tells a story. We're here to make gifting easier, faster, and more beautiful with every petal, every time.

From Petals to Powerful Platform

It started with a simple idea sending flowers should be as easy as sending a text. Tulipink was born to bridge tradition and technology, helping people express love, gratitude, and celebration with just a few taps. Today, we proudly deliver thousands of fresh bouquets to doorsteps across the country.

Our Purpose, Promise, and Path Forward

Goal

Our goal is to make flower gifting effortless and emotional by combining fast delivery, a smooth app experience, and thoughtful gift options. We’re committed to expanding our reach and continuously improving to deliver joy with every order.

Mission

We envision a world where sending flowers is as effortless as sending a message quick, meaningful, and always memorable. Tulipink strives to become the most trusted and loved flower delivery platform, known not just for speed, but for the emotion and elegance behind every order.

Vision

At Tulipink, our mission is to make the act of gifting flowers simple, joyful, and deeply personal. We aim to blend the beauty of fresh blooms with the power of modern technology, helping people express their emotions in the most heartfelt way whether it's love, celebration, care, or comfort.

Global Presence, Unified Passion

We are a diversified team mainly based in the United States. Some code, some write; some are time masters, some are fighting with procrastination. What brings us together is the deep belief in the art of time management; the passion to build a simple, but not simpler product; and the goal to help everyone boost productivity and enjoy life.

In as early as 2010, the founding team began the endeavor with an Android app called GTasks, which syncs with Google Tasks. At that time, the app was listed as "one of the best todo list apps" at Google Play Store. In 2013, we officially launched TickTick. This new app derives from GTasks, but has a lot more functionalities and can sync across multiple platforms.
